關於我們

我們是一群在 University of California (UC)各大分校工程學院和電腦科系大學部與研究所就讀的學生。秉持國家積極發展STEM科學,技術，工程，數學教育的理念，積極協助我們社區的青少年學習各項最新知識。因應當今迅速發展的人工智能，雲端計算，超級電腦與萬用機器人的趨勢， 夏令營將以簡潔有趣， 生動活潑的方式進行， 帶領青少年進入 可預知的未來世界 ，並全力發掘其自身未知的潛力 。與教於樂中 ，開啟探索之旅。

About Us

We're a young non-profit organization aimed at helping give tools to young students to embrace their passion for Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths in general. Our goal is to promote STEM to students all over our Community. Our main program is Artificial Intelligence, Computer Programming, Robotics Design and Construction, Mobile APP and 3D Printing. The summer camp will be conducted in a simple, interesting and lively way, leading young people into the foreseeable future world and striving to explore their own unknown potential. And teach in the interesting, open the journey of exploration.
